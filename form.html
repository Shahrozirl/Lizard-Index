Before contacting the REPTI team, please keep the following points in mind. While we don't like stating the obvious, experience has shown that sharing these notes can help save everyone time:

REPTI is an educational resource dedicated to reptile care. We are not a pet store, breeder, veterinary clinic, or supplier of reptile products. Unless otherwise mentioned in a specific collaboration or article, we do not sell reptiles or reptile-related items.

Our team does not offer individual pet health consultations, veterinary advice, or personalized husbandry troubleshooting. While we strive to provide accurate, science-backed information, any health concerns about your pet should be addressed with a qualified reptile veterinarian.

Choosing the right reptile for you is a personal decision. We cannot tell you which species to get, what your setup should cost, or what fits best with your lifestyle. However, we encourage you to explore our care guides and articles to learn about each species, and to join reptile forums or online communities where experienced keepers can share their insights and advice.

MEET THE AUTHOR

My name is Dr. Skye Varanus, professional reptile husbandry researcher and consultant. I have kept a variety of exotic pets for most of my life, but I’ve worked with reptiles specifically for over 10 years.
