MEET THE AUTHOR

My name is Dr. Skye Varanus, professional reptile husbandry researcher and consultant. I have kept a variety of exotic pets for most of my life, but I’ve worked with reptiles specifically for over 10 years. I’m currently working on my master’s degree in natural history-based exotic animal husbandry from West Liberty University!

I find reptiles so wonderfully captivating, challenging, and diverse that I’ve dedicated my career to understanding them better. That’s how REPTI was born!

What is REPTI? REPTI is a collection of science-backed reptile care resources from top experts around the world, all in one place. You’ll find in-depth care manuals, quick reference sheets, product reviews, helpful directories, and the occasional blog post.
