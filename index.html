Our Story

The foundation of The Reptile Repository started in 2025 with Shahroz Khalid (Thats me!). Reading reviews upon reviews can get boring real quick. Some websites only include information but reading it all can get tiring, others would fill their websites with ads and the reading experience would get tarnished.

Here at The Reptile Repository, we strive to be just that, A repository, a safe space, an escape for all things reptiles. Our website has amazing visuals while still touching on all the technicals, (such as habitats/food etc), giving you all the information you'll be looking for.
