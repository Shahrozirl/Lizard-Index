About REPTI

Welcome to REPTI, your ultimate destination for trusted, science-backed reptile care education. Our mission is simple but vital: to raise the standard of reptile husbandry worldwide through accurate, accessible, and practical information. Whether you're a brand-new reptile owner or a seasoned enthusiast, REPTI is your go-to guide for building healthy environments and meaningful relationships with your scaly companions.

Reptiles are some of the most captivating and misunderstood creatures on Earth. From the ancient elegance of the Green Iguana to the curious charm of the Leopard Gecko, each species carries its own set of needs and behaviors. Unfortunately, misinformation and outdated care practices are still common. That’s why REPTI was created — to cut through the myths and provide modern, evidence-based resources that empower keepers to do better.

Our content is rooted in biology, ecology, and the latest herpetocultural research. Every care guide, tip, and product recommendation is carefully compiled by experienced reptile keepers, hobbyists, and husbandry researchers. We don't just tell you what to do — we explain the why behind it, giving you the knowledge to make informed decisions for your reptile's wellbeing.

At REPTI, we believe that reptile care is more than a hobby — it’s a responsibility. Reptiles are not decorations or low-maintenance pets; they are intelligent, sentient beings with complex needs. When you bring one into your home, you're making a commitment to support a living creature’s physical and psychological health for many years to come. We take that responsibility seriously — and we’re here to help you every step of the way.

Our website includes detailed species-specific care guides, shopping lists, habitat setup instructions, lighting and heating advice, feeding recommendations, and much more. Whether you're researching the humidity requirements of a Chinese Water Dragon, choosing the right UVB bulb for a Uromastyx, or preparing a bioactive setup for your Bearded Dragon — we’ve got you covered.

In addition to care sheets, we publish educational blog articles, product reviews, and tips on ethical reptile keeping. We’re continuously updating our resources to reflect new discoveries, technologies, and best practices in herpetology and reptile care.

Behind REPTI is a passionate team dedicated to improving the lives of reptiles through education and advocacy. We are hobbyists, researchers, educators, and above all, reptile lovers. Our goal is to build a global community of responsible reptile keepers who value science, empathy, and progress.

Thank you for being here. By choosing to learn more, you're already making a difference in your reptile’s life. Together, we can transform reptile care from guesswork into confident, compassionate stewardship.

Welcome to the REPTI family — where better reptile care begins.
